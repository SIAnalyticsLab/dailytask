💰 SI Analytics 30-Day Data Visualization Challenge — Day 4

Finance Data: Investment Habits during COVID-19 Lockdown
📁 Dataset: Finance_data.csv

🧩 Background

Today’s dataset, “Finance_data.csv”, was collected via Google Forms as a fun research project during the COVID-19 lockdown. It captures individuals’ investment behavior, preferences, and financial attitudes across different age groups and genders.

You’ll use this dataset to uncover how people invest, what motivates their choices, and what they value most in their investment decisions.

📄 Dataset name: Finance_data.csv

Key variables include:
VariableDescription
GENDERMale/Female
AGERespondent age
Investment_AvenuesWhether the person invests (Yes/No)
Mutual_Funds, Equity_Market, Debentures, etc.Ranking of preferred investment options
Stock_MarketWhether the person invests in the stock market
FactorKey factors influencing investment (e.g., Returns, Risk)
ObjectiveInvestment goal (e.g., Capital Appreciation, Income)

🎯 Daily Task Objective

Develop an Investment Insights Dashboard showing investor preferences, objectives, and behavior patterns during the pandemic period.

💡 Core Question: “How do age, gender, and investment motivations influence people’s financial choices?”

🧮 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Load and Inspect the Data
    • R: read_csv("Finance_data.csv")
    • Python: pd.read_csv("Finance_data.csv")
    • Power BI: Home → Get Data → Text/CSV → Select the file
    Check for missing or inconsistent entries (e.g., Yes/No casing, spaces in column names). Rename columns where necessary for easier use (e.g., replace spaces with underscores).
  2. Clean and Prepare
    • Convert categorical variables (e.g., GENDER, Investment_Avenues) to factors or categories.
    • Standardize rankings (1 = highest preference).
    • Group or summarize the data to explore:
    o % of respondents investing in each avenue
    o Gender-wise differences in investment behavior
    o Common investment goals and durations
  3. Build Your Dashboard
    Visualize key insights using R, Python, or Power BI.
    Include at least four components:
    • 👩‍💼 Bar Chart: Top 5 preferred investment options by ranking
    • 📊 Pie Chart: Gender distribution of investors
    • 🎯 Stacked Bar Chart: Investment objective by gender (e.g., Capital Appreciation vs Growth)
    • 📆 Column Chart: Preferred investment duration by age group
    • 💸 Donut Chart (optional): Expected return percentages
    💡 Power BI Tip: Use “Investment_Avenues” as a slicer to filter across visuals.
    💡 R/Python Tip: Use Plotly or ggplot2 for interactivity and clean presentation.
  4. Add Summary Insights or Value Cards
    Show KPIs such as:
    • Total respondents
    • % who invest in stock market
    • Top investment avenue overall
    • Average expected return range
